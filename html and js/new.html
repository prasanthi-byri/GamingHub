About this Game

Picture Puzzle is a relaxing jigsaw puzzle game to put all the pieces together to create correct images. Picture Puzzles includes a huge number of attractive images of different difficulty levels from easy to hard.Your puzzle skills will be put to the test with this new pie-shaped puzzle game. Fit the pieces neatly in place, and create cute images.

Rating :

Instructions
  • Solve the given puzzle to make the perfect image
  • Swap the image slide with any image slide not only with white slide
  • The number of turns limited are 10. If it exceeds it leads to the failure of challenge
  • There is caution timer of 15 minutes which reminds you to relax. Press start to start the caution timer.
  • After completion of timer it reminds and you can press quit to exit the game or you can press start to continue the time so the time caution timer will start again.

About this Game

Archery is the sport, practice, or skill of using a bow to shoot arrows.A person who practices archery is typically called an archer, bowman, or toxophilite. Archery has been used for hunting and combat. In modern times, it is mainly a competitive sport and recreational activity.Archery, one of the oldest sports still practiced, is closely linked to the development of civilisation. As a cultural advance, it was comparable to the discovery of fire and the invention of the wheel.

Rating :

Instructions
  • Shoot the bow with the given arrows. For shooting press the touch pad once on the bow location where you want to shoot.
  • You will gain the score when the arrow touches the pole according to the following:
    • You will gain 7 points when the arrow touches the middle of the pole
    • You will gain 5 points when the arrow touches the location nearer to the middle of the pole
    • You will gain 3 points when the arrow touches the location nearer to the edges of the pole
    • You will gain 2 points when the arrow touches the location is closest nearer to the edges of the pole
    • You will gain 3 points when the arrow touches the edges of the pole
  • The number of arrows limited are 7. If it exceeds it leads to the failure of challenge
  • There is caution timer of 15 minutes which reminds you to relax. Press start to start the caution timer.
  • After completion of timer it reminds and you can press quit to exit the game or you can press start to continue the time so the time caution timer will start again.
